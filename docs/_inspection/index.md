## üîç Inspection #1: Pre-Pour Foundation

**Timing**: Before pouring base slab concrete  
**Inspector**: Self or building inspector  
**Duration**: 30-60 minutes

### Foundation Checklist

#### Excavation & Bearing Soil
- [ ] Excavation dimensions correct (9' √ó 3' √ó 1.5' deep minimum)
- [ ] Reached firm bearing soil (no soft spots, organics, or mud)
- [ ] Bottom level within ¬±1"
- [ ] No standing water or wet areas
- [ ] Side slopes stable

**Pass Criteria**: Firm soil that forms a ball when squeezed but isn't muddy

#### AB3 Aggregate Base
- [ ] Vapor barrier installed on bearing soil
- [ ] AB3 material correct (well-graded crushed stone)
- [ ] Installed in three 4" lifts
- [ ] Each lift compacted separately
- [ ] **CRITICAL**: Compaction test shows ‚â•95% Modified Proctor
- [ ] Final surface level within ¬±1/2"
- [ ] Thickness correct (12" total)

**Pass Criteria**: Compaction test documentation showing 95%+ (REQUIRED!)

#### Ground Anchors
- [ ] Quantity correct (6 anchors)
- [ ] Material: Fiberglass #4 rebar, 8' long
- [ ] Driven to 6' minimum depth
- [ ] Spacing correct (24" along length)
- [ ] Rows at 6" and 18" from front edge
- [ ] Plumb (within 1/4" per foot)
- [ ] Protruding 2' above AB3
- [ ] Not damaged during installation

**Pass Criteria**: All anchors at correct depth and spacing

#### Base Slab Rebar
- [ ] All bars are #4 Grade 60 (check markings)
- [ ] Quantity correct:
  - [ ] R1: 2 longitudinal bars @ 9'
  - [ ] R2: 8 transverse bars @ 3'
- [ ] Rebar spacing correct (12" o.c. for transverse)
- [ ] Concrete cover: 3" minimum on all sides
- [ ] Rebar chairs at 3" height (not wood blocks!)
- [ ] All intersections tied with wire
- [ ] Bars clean (no loose rust, mud, or oil)
- [ ] No damaged or bent bars

**Pass Criteria**: Use cover meter or measure - 3" minimum cover is CRITICAL

#### Bearing Plates
- [ ] Quantity: 2 plates
- [ ] Size: 12" √ó 12" √ó 1/2" thick (A36 steel)
- [ ] Position Plate 1: 30" from left edge, 18" from front
- [ ] Position Plate 2: 66" from left edge, 18" from front
- [ ] Elevation: 36" above AB3 (top of wall height)
- [ ] Both plates level (within 1/16")
- [ ] Elevated on chairs/supports (not sitting on AB3)
- [ ] Wired to rebar to prevent movement

**Pass Criteria**: Laser level shows plates at exact same elevation ¬±1/16"

#### Drainage Pipes
- [ ] Quantity: 2 pipes (4" PVC Schedule 40)
- [ ] Position 1: 36" from left edge
- [ ] Position 2: 72" from left edge
- [ ] Front elevation: 6" above AB3
- [ ] Back elevation: 3.6" above AB3 (2% slope)
- [ ] Slope toward creek side (confirmed with level)
- [ ] Supported to prevent movement
- [ ] Pipes clean inside (no debris)

**Pass Criteria**: 2% minimum slope confirmed with level

## üîç Inspection #2: Pre-Pour Wall

**Timing**: After base cured 7 days, before pouring wall  
**Inspector**: Self or building inspector  
**Duration**: 30-45 minutes

### Wall Inspection Checklist

#### Base Slab Condition
- [ ] Cured minimum 7 days
- [ ] No significant cracks (hairline OK)
- [ ] Surface clean (no debris, dirt, oil)
- [ ] Top surface level
- [ ] Bearing plates at correct elevation
- [ ] Test cylinders available for review

**Pass Criteria**: Compressive strength ‚â•2,000 PSI after 7 days

#### Wall Vertical Rebar (R3 & R4)
- [ ] Quantity correct:
  - [ ] R3: 8 front verticals @ 3'
  - [ ] R4: 8 back verticals @ 3'
- [ ] Spacing: 12" on center along 8' length
- [ ] Bottom lap: 6" minimum into base
- [ ] Vertical through wall: 24"
- [ ] Top hook: 6" horizontal
- [ ] All bars Grade 60 #4
- [ ] Concrete cover: 3" on all faces
- [ ] Bars plumb (within 1/4" per foot)
- [ ] No damaged bars

**Pass Criteria**: Spacing accurate, proper lap lengths, correct cover

#### Wall Horizontal Rebar (R5)
- [ ] Quantity: 16 bars (8 per level √ó 2 levels)
- [ ] Length: 3' each
- [ ] Level 1 elevation: ~12" above base (mid-wall)
- [ ] Level 2 elevation: ~30" above base (near top)
- [ ] Spacing: 12" o.c. along length
- [ ] Hooks: 6" at each end, wrapping verticals
- [ ] All intersections with verticals tied
- [ ] Concrete cover maintained

**Pass Criteria**: Horizontal bars at correct elevations, properly tied

#### Wall Forms
- [ ] Form material adequate (2√ó6 or 2√ó8 minimum)
- [ ] Form height: 24" (2 feet)
- [ ] Forms plumb (check with level)
- [ ] Adequate bracing (concrete is heavy!)
- [ ] No gaps in forms (seal with tape if needed)
- [ ] Forms oiled for easy removal
- [ ] Snap ties or spreaders at 24" spacing
- [ ] Forms secure at base (won't blow out)

**Pass Criteria**: Forms plumb within 1/4" per 10 feet

## üîç Inspection #3: Final Inspection

**Timing**: Minimum 14 days after wall pour, before loading  
**Inspector**: Building inspector (official)  
**Duration**: 45-90 minutes

### Final Inspection Checklist

#### Curing & Strength
- [ ] Minimum 14-day cure at 70¬∞F
- [ ] Extended cure if cold weather
- [ ] Test cylinders broken and reviewed
- [ ] Compressive strength ‚â•3,000 PSI
- [ ] Documentation available

**Pass Criteria**: Test cylinders show 3,000 PSI minimum at 28 days

#### Concrete Quality
- [ ] No significant cracks (>1/16" width)
- [ ] No honeycombing or voids
- [ ] Proper consolidation around rebar
- [ ] Surface finish acceptable
- [ ] No spalling or deterioration
- [ ] Bearing plates fully embedded
- [ ] Bearing plates at correct elevation

**Pass Criteria**: Sound concrete with no structural defects

#### Dimensional Accuracy
- [ ] Overall length: 8'-0" ¬±1/4"
- [ ] Overall depth: 2'-0" ¬±1/4"
- [ ] Overall height: 3'-0" ¬±1/4"
- [ ] Wall plumb: within 1/4" per 10'
- [ ] Bearing plate spacing: 36" ¬±1/4"
- [ ] Bearing plates level: ¬±1/8"
- [ ] Bearing plate elevation: correct for beam height

**Pass Criteria**: All dimensions within tolerance

#### Drainage System
- [ ] Pipes functional and clear
- [ ] Water flows through pipes
- [ ] 2% slope maintained
- [ ] Outlets clear and accessible
- [ ] No clogs or obstructions
- [ ] Screens/grates installed

**Pass Criteria**: Water flows freely through drainage system

#### Backfill & Grading
- [ ] Backfill properly compacted
- [ ] No voids behind wall
- [ ] Surface graded away from wall
- [ ] No ponding water near abutment
- [ ] Approach area stable

**Pass Criteria**: Proper compaction and drainage

#### Safety & Access
- [ ] Site cleaned up
- [ ] No tripping hazards
- [ ] Temporary barriers if needed
- [ ] Access clear for beam installation
- [ ] Forms removed and disposed of

**Pass Criteria**: Site safe for beam installation

## ‚ö†Ô∏è Common Inspection Failures

### Top 10 Reasons Abutments Fail Inspection

<div style="background: #f8d7da; border-left: 4px solid #dc3545; padding: 1rem; margin: 1rem 0;">
<h4 style="margin-top: 0;">‚ùå #1: Insufficient Concrete Cover</h4>
<p><strong>Required:</strong> 3" minimum<br>
<strong>Common mistake:</strong> Using wood blocks instead of proper chairs<br>
<strong>Fix:</strong> Remeasure before pour, use correct height chairs</p>
</div>

<div style="background: #f8d7da; border-left: 4px solid #dc3545; padding: 1rem; margin: 1rem 0;">
<h4 style="margin-top: 0;">‚ùå #2: Poor AB3 Compaction</h4>
<p><strong>Required:</strong> 95% Modified Proctor<br>
<strong>Common mistake:</strong> No compaction test<br>
<strong>Fix:</strong> Hire testing company ($100-200)</p>
</div>

<div style="background: #f8d7da; border-left: 4px solid #dc3545; padding: 1rem; margin: 1rem 0;">
<h4 style="margin-top: 0;">‚ùå #3: Bearing Plates Misaligned</h4>
<p><strong>Required:</strong> Exact position ¬±1/4"<br>
<strong>Common mistake:</strong> Not using laser level<br>
<strong>Fix:</strong> Rent laser level, measure three times</p>
</div>

<div style="background: #f8d7da; border-left: 4px solid #dc3545; padding: 1rem; margin: 1rem 0;">
<h4 style="margin-top: 0;">‚ùå #4: Inadequate Lap Splices</h4>
<p><strong>Required:</strong> 6" minimum into base<br>
<strong>Common mistake:</strong> Verticals not lapped enough<br>
<strong>Fix:</strong> Ensure 6" lap before wall pour</p>
</div>

<div style="background: #f8d7da; border-left: 4px solid #dc3545; padding: 1rem; margin: 1rem 0;">
<h4 style="margin-top: 0;">‚ùå #5: Premature Loading</h4>
<p><strong>Required:</strong> 14 days minimum cure<br>
<strong>Common mistake:</strong> Loading after 7 days<br>
<strong>Fix:</strong> Wait for test cylinders, minimum 14 days</p>
