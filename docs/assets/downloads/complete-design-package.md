## üéì KEY CONSTRUCTION TIPS

### Rebar Installation
1. **Use proper chairs**: Concrete chairs maintain 3" cover - don't use wood blocks!
2. **Tie everything**: Wire-tie all rebar intersections - prevents movement during pour
3. **Check cover twice**: Measure cover before and after tying - #1 inspection failure
4. **Clean rebar**: Remove loose rust, mud, oil - affects bond strength

### Concrete Placement
1. **Order extra**: Get 2.0 CY even though you need 1.78 - allows for waste/spillage
2. **Vibrate properly**: Insert vibrator every 18-24" - eliminates air pockets
3. **Don't overwork**: Stop vibrating when large air bubbles stop rising
4. **Weather matters**: 
   - Ideal: 50-80¬∞F
   - Hot weather (>85¬∞F): Wet curing essential, may need evaporation retarder
   - Cold weather (<40¬∞F): Protect from freezing, extend cure time

### Critical Mistakes to Avoid
1. ‚ùå **Insufficient AB3 compaction** ‚Üí Settlement ‚Üí Catastrophic failure
2. ‚ùå **Poor concrete cover** ‚Üí Rust ‚Üí Structural weakness in 5-10 years
3. ‚ùå **Bearing plates misaligned** ‚Üí Eccentric loading ‚Üí Cracking
4. ‚ùå **Premature loading** ‚Üí Cracking ‚Üí Reduced strength
5. ‚ùå **Inadequate vibration** ‚Üí Honeycombing ‚Üí Weak structure
