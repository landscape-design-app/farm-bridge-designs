**Preparation Steps (Day 4 - separate from other bending):**

4. **Wrap NO BOND half:**
   ```

   - Clear packing tape
   - Yellow spray paint

   a) Start at center mark
   b) Wrap tightly, overlapping 50%
   c) Continue to end (12" section)
   d) Secure with clear tape
   e) Spray wrapped section with yellow paint
   f) Label "NO BOND" with marker
   g) Let dry
   ```
